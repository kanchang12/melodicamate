Privacy Policy — MelodicaMate

Effective date: 30 Dec 2025

MelodicaMate (“the app”) is an early-stage learning tool that helps users practice music with real-time feedback. We collect as little data as possible.

Microphone (Audio Input)

The app requires microphone access to analyze live playing or singing. Raw audio is not stored by default.

Local Device Storage

The app stores settings and practice summaries only on your device (e.g., key/notation choice, session note events, daily timer).

Data sent to server (optional)

When you request AI coaching or voice feedback, the app may send structured non-audio data (note events/metrics). No raw audio is required.

Third-party services

Google Gemini may generate coaching text and ElevenLabs may generate voice output, using only the minimum data needed.
